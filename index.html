Our Story

Made with love,
served with pride.

Parantha & Wraps was born from a simple idea — to bring the warmth of a Punjabi home kitchen to the streets of London. Every parantha is rolled by hand, every wrap is built with care, and every bite tells a story.

We use fresh dough every morning, source our spices from trusted suppliers, and never compromise on the stuff that matters. No shortcuts, no freezer packs — just real food, real fast.
